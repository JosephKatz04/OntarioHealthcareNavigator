Settlement.Org describes its Health section as information for newcomers about health insurance, finding a doctor or other medical professional, and health services for people and families.

The Health section links to topics including OHIP and health insurance, finding a doctor or medical professional, community and public health, mental health and addiction, emergency services, nutrition and healthy living, sexual and reproductive health, family health, refugee health, patients' rights and responsibilities, and disability.

The emergency services section includes newcomer-oriented information about hospital emergency department visits, getting help in an emergency, and finding a hospital.

Do not use this summary as official government guidance. Use Settlement.Org as a newcomer-friendly support source and verify healthcare rules against official Ontario sources.
