The official Ontario page explains how to find a family doctor, nurse practitioner, or specialist, and includes information about the Health Care Connect program.

The page says a family doctor or nurse practitioner is a primary health care clinician for new, non-emergency health concerns.

The page lists two options for finding a family doctor or nurse practitioner who is accepting new patients: register with Health Care Connect, or contact an Ontario physician to ask if they are accepting new patients.

The page says Health Care Connect can search for a family doctor or nurse practitioner accepting new patients in a person’s community. It also says joining Health Care Connect does not guarantee that a clinician will be found immediately.

Do not use this summary to decide eligibility or availability. The exact program requirements, contact methods, and current timelines must be read from the official page.
